About Me

Master's candidate in International Trade & Environment at Paris 1 Panthéon-Sorbonne, trained in econometrics, applied macroeconomics, and data-driven economic analysis. My work focuses on identifying causal relationships in complex economic systems and translating large-scale datasets into structured, decision-relevant insights using rigorous quantitative methods.

I also approach cross-border strategy and sustainability-related policy problems through structured analytical frameworks, integrating market dynamics, regulatory constraints, and operational considerations. My focus is on producing clear, evidence-based reasoning in ambiguous, data-intensive environments to support strategic decision-making.
